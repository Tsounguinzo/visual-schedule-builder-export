Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 17, 2025

Information Collection and Use

The Visual Schedule Builder Export extension only accesses the schedule data displayed in Concordia's Visual Schedule Builder when you click the export button. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

Google Calendar Access

The extension requires permission to add events to your Google Calendar. This permission is used solely for creating course events based on your schedule.

Data Storage

No data is stored by the extension. Schedule information is processed temporarily in your browser only during the export process.

Third-Party Services

The extension interacts with Google Calendar API to create events. Please refer to Google's privacy policy for information about how they handle your data.

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page.
